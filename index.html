Skincare & body care
Your full routine
Face · body · shower · hair — all mapped to your week
Morning routine — after your AM shower, or after your post-gym shower on training days. Do your face routine after you've stepped out and towel-dried.
1
Dokdo Cleanser double cleanse if you wore SPF overnight
Dokdo Cleansing Oil first if needed, then Cleanser. Otherwise just the cleanser alone in the AM.
2
Equalberry Swimming Pool Toner AM only
Pat in while skin is still slightly damp. Preps and brightens — this is your AM toner only.
3
Innisfree Green Tea Vitamin C Serum
Your antioxidant protection for the day. Press in gently — don't rub.
4
Topical Faded Serum wait 1–2 min after Vit C
Hyperpigmentation treatment. Works synergistically with Vitamin C in the morning.
5
Bubble Vitamin C Eye Cream
Tap gently around the orbital bone with your ring finger — never drag.
6
Purito Bamboo Panthenol Cream
Lightweight AM moisturiser. Lighter finish under SPF than the Atobarrier — save that one for PM.
7
Sunscreen never skip
Last step every single morning. Vitamin C, Faded, Finacea and glycolic all increase photosensitivity — SPF is non-negotiable.
Evening routine — after your evening shower, or standalone if no second shower. Alternate Finacea and Retinol — never use both on the same night.
1
Dokdo Cleansing Oil → Dokdo Cleanser
Full double cleanse every evening. Oil cleanser dissolves SPF and buildup; water-based cleanser cleans the skin itself.
2
Byoma Milky Toner PM only
Pat in while damp. Barrier-supporting and soothing — primes skin before actives.
3
Beta-Glucan Barrier Serum PM only
Calming and barrier-repairing. Let it absorb fully before adding actives.
4a
Finacea 15% Gel alternate nights
Azelaic acid for hyperpigmentation and clarity. Use on nights you are NOT using retinol. Thin layer — don't overdo it.
4b
Medik8 Retinol alternate nights
Use on nights you are NOT using Finacea. Never both on the same night. If skin feels sensitised, take a rest night.
5
Bubble Vitamin C Eye Cream
Fine AM and PM both.
6
Aestura Atobarrier365 Cream PM only
Richer than the Purito — seals evening actives and supports overnight barrier repair.
AM body — every day post-shower
1
Olay Vitamin C Body Lotion
Apply to damp skin immediately after towel drying — boosts absorption and locks in moisture. Every morning without exception.
2
Saltair AHA Deodorant
After the lotion has absorbed. The AHA works on underarm texture and darkening over time — a smart multitasker.
PM body — 2–3 nights per week
1
The Ordinary Glycolic Acid Toner on non-glycolic-wash days only
Apply to clean dry skin on legs, arms, décolletage. Never use on days you've already used the Glycolic Acid body wash in the shower.
2
Saltair Retinol Body Lotion 2–3x/week · PM only
After glycolic has absorbed (5 min). Upper arms, thighs, décolletage. Don't use on glycolic-wash days either.
3
Saltair Body Butter every night
On active nights: after retinol. On rest nights: straight after Olay if you need extra moisture. Use every single night.
Body wash rotation — use these deliberately, not randomly. The Dove bar is for sensitive areas only. Your gym showers count — use the right wash on the right day.
A
Naturium Glycolic Acid Body Wash 2–3x/week max
Your exfoliating wash. Use on days when you will NOT also apply TO Glycolic Acid toner or Retinol lotion in the evening. Great for texture, KP and body hyperpigmentation.
B
Naturium Glow Getter Multi-Oil Body Wash alternate days
Your nourishing non-exfoliating wash. Use on days you plan to apply TO Glycolic Acid or Retinol body lotion in the evening — so you're not layering exfoliation.
C
Dove Beauty Bar targeted use only
Sensitive and intimate areas only. The active washes are too strong for daily use there.

Non-negotiable rules
!
Never double-exfoliate the body on the same day
Glycolic body wash + TO Glycolic toner on the same day = over-exfoliation. The weekly schedule alternates these deliberately — trust the system.
!
Never use Finacea and Retinol on the same night
Both are potent actives. If skin feels sensitised, take a rest night with just the barrier serum and Atobarrier — let your skin breathe.
!
SPF every single morning — zero exceptions
You're using Vitamin C, Faded, Finacea and glycolic. Skipping SPF actively undoes your hyperpigmentation work.
→
Apply body lotion and glycolic to damp (not wet) skin
Olay lotion and TO Glycolic absorb best on slightly damp skin — within 2 minutes of stepping out. Retinol lotion and Body Butter go on fully dry skin.
→
Don't wash hair more than twice a week
Twice weekly is the sweet spot for relaxed 4C hair. More risks moisture loss and weakening the line of demarcation.
→
Dove bar is for targeted areas only
The active washes are not appropriate for sensitive or intimate areas. Keep the Dove bar just for those.
